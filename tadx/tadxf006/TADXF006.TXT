I took these pictures when i was bored at work, we got some canon-ion camera,
where you can insert disks. (50 pictures on one disk). 

3phones.jpg  An overview of three telephone booths, with in the middle some
             illegal person holding a paper for his face. Taken somewhere
             in the netherlands. (wont be a secret for people who know
             something about dutch busses).

all-1.jpg    This is a picture of the latest telephones the dutch ptt uses.
             They can be found every where in the Netherlands. And the dutch
             ptt claims they put 10 new ones every day. Above the telephone
             you can see part of a list, where the country direct numbers
             are written down and some other 'important' notes in some
             languages. At the top of the telephone you can see a slot where
             you can insert telephone chip-cards. Beneath it there is a 
             display which tells you to insert a card and dial a number. And 
             another display with some other information. Beneath the keypad 
             you can insert magnetic stripe passes.

all-2.jpg    Same one as all-1.jpg only picture is taking sideways.

all-3c.jpg   Same telephone as all-1.jpg and all-2.jpg. Its a close up of the
             upper part. You can read the display which says Insert Card and
             choose number (in dutch).

all-4c.jpg   Another closup of the bottem part. You can see the keypad, the
             part where you can insert magnetic-stripe cards and some buttons, 
             where you can select the display language, volume, eject the 
             phone card and some special functions.

munt-1.jpg   A picture of an older telphone where you can insert coins (in
             the upper right). Beneath the keypad is a button for the
             loudness. On the bottem you get your money back (or what is left
             of it). 

munt-2.jpg   Another old coin-telepone where you can insert different coins.

slot.jpg     Picture of a telephone you can rent from the dutch ptt. They got
             it covered with metal and stuff. Only the keypad and the mouth
             piece is really part of the telephone.
