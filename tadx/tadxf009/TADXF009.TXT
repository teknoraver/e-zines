  It took some time to complete another Arrested Development X-FiLE. This is 
Issue #009. It was released 5th of August 1995. This Issue covers interesting 
WWW sites on the internet. Most of the sites were taken from a paper magazine
called Telecommagazine (dutch). The Magazine was released in April 1995 and 
June 1995. If you know of any other interesting sites, just give me a call
on Arrested Development or email me at omega@via.nl. I'm currently working
on setting up the bbs on internet, i'll keep you informed about what the
adress and login procedure will be.

3Com Corporation : http://www.3com.com/
        Everything about 3Com's high performance scalable networking.
        Extended technical information about their products and a looking
        for work databank.

ADC Telecommunications : http://www.adc.com
        All ADC Products on the topic of networking and transmission.

Apple Computer : http:/www.apple.com
        This server is subdivided into the next subjects : general
        information about Apple, products and support, services and
        products for programmers, research and technology programs, 
        special products and programs from others.

AT&T : http://www.att.com
        You can look at the year results of 1994. Furthermore you get
        a summary of products and services. Press-messages are updated
        weekly.

AT&T Global Information Solutions : http://ncrinfo.ncr.com/
        On this server there is information about Waven from NCR. And 
        a summary of financial services from AT&T credit-GIS.

ATM Forum : http://www.atmforum.com/
        The ATM Forum is a worldwide organisation to stimulate the use of
        ATM. You can find their newsletter, documentation and training
        software.

Bellsouth Telecommunications : http://www.bst.bls.com/
        The very nice graphical access screen is subdivided into the next
        topics: Who are we, news, applications, products and services and
        community crossroads. The topic products and services tells you 
        all about speech, data, video and other services.

Borland : http:/www.borland.com/
        Latest news, company info, technical info, product info, programs
        & services, feedback and the Files.

BT-Net : http://www.bt.net/
        BT Internet-services like Direct Plus, ISDN, Premium Dial, Relay
        and SMDS. Also mailboxes and consultancy. The access page consists
        of : BTnet help, Why choose BTnet?, more about BTnet Connectivity.

Cabletron : http:/www.ctron.com/
        Cabletron opens with news like information aboiut Synthesis. Also
        Cabletron in the news, product catalog, service & technical support,
        customer profiles, technical papers, LanCare, Seminars & Training
        courses and talk to us.

Cisco Systems : http://cisco.com/
        You have to login as guest. The server is build out of three
        topics : what's new, product information, software image library
        and service and support.

Compaq Computer Corporation : http://www.compaq.com/
        The information about compaq consists of : overview, what's new,
        product information, service and support and additional information.

CyberCash : http://www.cybercash.com/
        The CyberCash payingsystem is designed to do transactions electronical
        via the internet.

Data General : http://www.dg.com
        Data General delivers servers and storage products. The access page
        : News & Events, company info, products, services, partners &
        sollutions, customers, shopping catalogs and common sense viewpoints.

Dell Computer Corporation : http://www.dell.com/
        The following topics : Corporate Information, products & Catalogs,
        technical support and international. Also information about the
        pentium chips.

        Digicash has info about paying electronical, like ecash. Also info
        about Digicash itself, publications, projects and products and
        a summary of smartcards like electronic purse, road toll card and
        facility management card.

        You can select the next topics : Company overview, what's new from
        digital, quickindex, new systems & Options catalog online, customer
        peridicals, service training and support, products & services,

        services, upcoming events.

        ethernet, ethernet software, technical papers and reports, trouble-
        shooting numbers.

        information.

GE Information Services : http://www.ge.com/geis/index.html

Hewlett-Packard : http:/www.hp.com/
        Information about the following topics : access guide, about HP,
