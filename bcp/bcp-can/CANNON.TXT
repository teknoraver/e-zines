                     -*- PVC Pipe Cannon -*-
           Making a PVC Pipe Cannon is very Simple, All you
           need is some PVC Pipe, a Cap for one end and a 
           Propane torch. Now here is how you assemble this
           knew toy of your. Just put the cap on the end of
           the cannon as you normally would and drill a hole 
           in it big enough for the nozzle of the torch to fit
           in. (Make sure the torch is a self ignition one) 
           get a towel and ball it up real tight, then put it
           down the barrel about half way to create good air
           pressure upon detontion. Now, put the projectile in
           the cannon and make sure it is right aginst the towel.
           Put the Nozzle of the Torch in the hole and begin
           to fill the cannon up with propane. After about 10-15
           seconds of relasing the gas, push the auto lighter button
           and boom. Something just went flying. I usally make 
           the bore of mine about the size of a Softball. 
           Softballs shoot great and with high speed because 
           they are a perfect fit. The last one i had broke a 
           piece of Plywood in two and the soft ball bounced 
           up in to the woods never to be seen again. So 
           if you want to scare some one, Just pull out your 
           cannon and fire away.


                                                -Lars
