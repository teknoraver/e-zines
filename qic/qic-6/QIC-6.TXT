When you talk on the cordless phone with somone, what you don't realize is
that you are actually talking over a "radio station" broadcasting your entire
conversation to your neighborhood.

With just about any police scanner, you can pull these frequencies out of the
air and hear them right in your own home.

How to Make Money by eavesdropping
ออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออ

I would change your setup so that you can record what comes through
your scanner, whether it be putting a recorder through the scanner like you
do with a telephone, or sitting a tape recorder next to the speaker.

        really weird and I knew I could make a easy buck off it.  So I

        she threatened, that didn't work, second she begged me, third, I
